Select the <action> from the <menu-options>.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Compare the <match> against the <expected-hash>.
Transform the <upper: uppercase> from the <message>.

Join the <sentence> from the <words>.

RESPONSE actions (data exits the feature set to the caller)
-----------------------------------------------------------
Return an <OK: status> with <user>.
Throw a <NotFound: error> for the <user>.

EXPORT actions (data persists, broadcasts, or is made globally visible)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Store the <user> into the <user-repository>.
Publish as <app-config> <config>.
Log <message> to the <console>.
Send the <welcome> to the <connection>.
Emit a <UserCreated: event> with <user>.
Notify the <recipient> with "Your order has shipped.".
Write the <report> to the <output-file>.
Append the <entry> to the <audit-log>.
Schedule the <heartbeat> with 30.

FILE actions (file system operations)
--------------------------------------
Make the <uploads> at the <uploads-path>.
Copy the <backup> to the <backup-destination>.
Move the <archive> to the <archive-path>.

TERMINAL actions (interactive terminal UI)
------------------------------------------
Clear the <display> for the <terminal>.
Show the <panel> for the <terminal>.
Render the <screen> to the <terminal>.
Repaint the <header> at the <terminal>.

SERVICE actions (long-running infrastructure)
---------------------------------------------
Start the <http-server> with <contract>.
Stop the <http-server> with <application>.
Listen the <incoming> on the <event-channel>.
Keepalive the <application> for the <events>.
Connect the <connection> to the <server-address>.
Broadcast the <notification> to the <socket-server>.
Close the <session> with <connection>.

---------------------------------
